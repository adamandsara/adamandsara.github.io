Information

Speeches

If you're Irish or German, you might be expecting a few speeches at the beginning or end of the meal by the best man or maybe the father of the bride.

But at a Swedish wedding, speeches are a whole thing! They happen throughout the meal. Friends, family and relatives might say a few words.

Our toastmasters Kristin Öster and Kevin Lynch will coordinate the speeches. Feel very free to hold a speech, if you want to! Contact them at adamandsara2025@gmail.com

Tal

Vill du hålla ett tal? Kontakta våra toastmasters Kristin Öster och Kevin Lynch på adamandsara2025@gmail.com

Breakfast, lunch and dinner

If you choose to arrive at the Friday, you are invited to have pizza with us at Rock Farm!

Breakfast can be bought when booking the accommodation. There is also the possibility to make your own breakfast in the kitchens at the Rock Farm area, if you bring ingredients. We will also put some ingredients there for a basic breakfast.

Lunch can be bought in the village of Slane, or you can prepare your own lunch in the kitchens at the area, if you bring ingredients.

Frukost, lunch och middag

Om du anländer på fredagen är du varmt välkommen att delta i en pizzamiddag på Rock Farm!

Frukost kan köpas när man bokar boende. Det finns också möjlighet att göra sin egen frukost i köken på området på Rock Farm, om man tar med egna ingredienser. Vi kommer också att ställa dit några basala ingredienser.

Lunch kan köpas i byn Slane, eller så kan man laga egen lunch i köken på området om man tar med ingredienser.
